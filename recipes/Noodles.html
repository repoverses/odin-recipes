Description

Stir-fried noodles are a quick, delicious, and versatile dish made with cooked noodles tossed in a flavorful sauce, crisp vegetables, and optional proteins like chicken, shrimp, or tofu. Popular in many Asian cuisines, noodles can be made spicy, savory, or even sweet-sour depending on your taste

This recipe is perfect for a weeknight dinner and great for using up leftover veggies in your fridge. Best of all, it comes together in under 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 200g (about 7 oz) noodles (egg noodles, ramen, or rice noodles)
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 small onion, sliced>
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce (optional)
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions and sesame seeds for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles according to package instructions. Drain and toss with a little oil to prevent sticking.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add garlic and onion. Stir-fry for 1–2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add carrots, bell pepper, and cabbage. Stir-fry until just tender but still crisp.
  5. Add the cooked noodles to the pan.
  6. Pour in soy sauce, chili sauce, and oyster sauce. Toss everything together to coat the noodles evenly.
  7. Stir-fry for another 2–3 minutes until heated through.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds if desired. Serve hot.
