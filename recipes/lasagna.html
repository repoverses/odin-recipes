Description

This hearty and delicious lasagna is a classic Italian favorite. Layered with rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella, it's the perfect comfort food for any night of the week.

Serve it hot and bubbly straight from the oven with a side of garlic bread or a fresh salad for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • lb ground beef
  • 1 jar mariana sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and paper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, cook ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add marinara sauce to the beef and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan, salt, and pepper.
  6. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, layer sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella. Repeat layers.
  7. Top with remaining mozzarella and cover with foil.
  8. Bake for 25 minutes, then uncover and bake for 15 more minutes until bubbly and golden.
  9. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
