Description

Homemade pizza is a fun and rewarding dish you can customize to your taste. Whether you're into a classic Margherita, pepperoni, veggie, or something more adventurous, pizza is always a crowd-pleaser.

This recipe uses a simple dough, rich tomato sauce, and lots of gooey mozzarella. You can bake it in a conventional oven and top it with anything you love — olives, mushrooms, onions, jalapeños, or fresh herbs.

Ingredients

  • For dough
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • For the Topping:
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce or marinara
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Olive oil for brushing the crust
  • Optional toppings: sliced onions, bell peppers, olives, mushrooms, pepperoni

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, yeast, sugar, and salt.
  2. Gradually add warm water and olive oil, mixing until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough for about 8–10 minutes until smooth. Cover and let it rise for 1 hour or until doubled.
  4. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  5. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your preferred thickness.
  6. Place it on a baking tray or pizza stone.
  7. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  8. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese generously, then add your favorite toppings.
  9. Brush edges with olive oil and sprinkle oregano.
  10. Bake for 12–15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbling.
  11. Garnish with fresh basil (optional) and serve hot.
